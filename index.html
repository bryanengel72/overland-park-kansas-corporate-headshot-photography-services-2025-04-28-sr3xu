Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my corporate headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that aligns with your industry. Dark blues, grays, and neutral tones work best. Avoid busy patterns, bright colors, and trendy styles that might quickly date your photos. We provide detailed wardrobe guidelines upon booking.

How long does a session take?

Standard sessions last 30-45 minutes, including time for outfit changes and reviewing photos. We recommend blocking out an hour to ensure a relaxed, unhurried experience. Executive packages with multiple looks may run longer.

What's included in the final delivery?

You'll receive professionally edited high-resolution digital files in both color and black & white, optimized versions for LinkedIn and social media, and a commercial use license. Rush delivery is available upon request.

Do you offer retouching services?

Yes, basic retouching is included with every session. This covers skin smoothing, removing temporary blemishes, and color correction. Advanced retouching services are available for an additional fee.

Can you accommodate group corporate shoots?

Absolutely! We specialize in corporate team photos and can arrange on-site sessions for groups of any size. Special pricing and scheduling options are available for corporate clients.
