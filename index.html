Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my corporate headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors that might distract from your face.

How long does a session take?

A typical corporate headshot session takes about 30 minutes, including time for outfit changes and reviewing photos.

When will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your professionally edited photos within 2-3 business days after your session.
